Terms and Conditions for Feedback Form Submission

Important Notice: By submitting this feedback form, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these terms and conditions. This form is part of an official Government of West Bengal initiative.

1. Data Collection and Usage

1.1 Information We Collect

When you submit feedback through this form, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, rank, phone number, email address (optional), and ZSB ID (if applicable)
  • Service Information: Branch affiliation and feedback content
  • Supporting Documents: Any files or images you choose to upload
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser information, and submission timestamp

1.2 Purpose of Data Collection

Your information is collected for the following purposes:

  1. Processing and responding to your feedback or grievances
  2. Improving services provided by West Bengal Sainik Board
  3. Maintaining records for administrative and legal compliance
  4. Statistical analysis for service enhancement (anonymized data only)
  5. Communication regarding your submission and follow-up actions

2. Data Protection and Security

2.1 Security Measures

The Government of West Bengal implements robust security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure data transmission using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Protected cloud storage infrastructure with government-grade security
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring
  • Backup and disaster recovery systems to prevent data loss

2.2 Data Storage and Retention

Storage Location: All data is stored within India in compliance with data localization requirements under Indian law.

  • Personal data is retained for a period of 7 years from submission date
  • Files and documents are stored securely in Google Drive with government controls
  • Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for statistical purposes
  • Data destruction follows secure deletion protocols after retention period

3. Legal Compliance and Framework

3.1 Applicable Laws

This data collection and processing complies with:

  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 - Primary data protection legislation
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 - Electronic data handling
  • Right to Information Act, 2005 - Government transparency requirements
  • West Bengal State Data Protection Guidelines, 2020 - State-specific requirements
  • Government of India IT Policy - Central government directives

3.2 Data Subject Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  1. Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data held by us
  2. Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  3. Right to Erasure: Request deletion of personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  4. Right to Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  5. Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)
  6. Right to Grievance Redressal: File complaints regarding data handling

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Internal Sharing

Your information may be shared within the West Bengal Sainik Board network:

  • Relevant ZSB branch offices for feedback processing
  • Administrative personnel for record-keeping and follow-up
  • IT support staff for technical maintenance (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Authorized government auditors for compliance verification

4.2 External Disclosure

No Commercial Sharing: Your personal information will never be sold, rented, or shared with commercial entities for marketing purposes.

Information may be disclosed to external parties only in the following circumstances:

  • As required by law, court order, or government directive
  • To relevant government departments for policy development
  • To law enforcement agencies in case of suspected illegal activities
  • With your explicit written consent for specific purposes

5. Technical Infrastructure and Processing

5.1 Third-Party Services

This feedback system utilizes the following trusted services:

  • Google Workspace for Government: For data storage and document management
  • Render Cloud Platform: For secure web hosting and form processing
  • Gmail for Business: For automated email notifications

All third-party services comply with government data protection standards and maintain appropriate security certifications.

5.2 File Upload Guidelines

  • Maximum file size: 10MB per file, 25MB total
  • Supported formats: Images (JPG, PNG), Documents (PDF, DOC, DOCX)
  • Files are scanned for security threats before storage
  • Inappropriate or malicious content will be rejected and reported

6. User Responsibilities and Obligations

6.1 Accurate Information

By submitting this form, you certify that:

  • All information provided is accurate and truthful to the best of your knowledge
  • You have the authority to submit feedback on behalf of yourself or your organization
  • You will not submit false, misleading, or defamatory content
  • You will not upload copyrighted material without proper authorization

6.2 Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Submitting spam, abusive, or offensive content
  • Attempting to hack, disrupt, or compromise system security
  • Using the form for commercial advertising or promotion
  • Submitting multiple identical or similar complaints
  • Uploading viruses, malware, or other harmful code

7.3 Complaint Resolution Process

  1. Initial Contact: Submit your complaint via email or phone
  2. Acknowledgment: Receive confirmation within 3 working days
  3. Investigation: Thorough review within 15 working days
  4. Resolution: Final response with action taken within 30 working days
  5. Appeal: Escalation to higher authorities if unsatisfied

  • Changes will be posted on this page with updated revision dates
  • Significant changes will be communicated via email to submitted contact addresses
  • Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of updated terms

9. Acceptance and Consent

By checking the acceptance checkbox and submitting the feedback form, you explicitly consent to:

  1. Collection and processing of your personal information as outlined above
  2. Storage of your data for the specified retention periods
  3. Sharing of information within the Sainik Board network as necessary
  4. Use of your feedback for service improvement initiatives
  5. Communication regarding your submission and follow-up actions

This consent is voluntary and you may withdraw it at any time by contacting the Data Protection Officer, subject to legal retention requirements for government records.
